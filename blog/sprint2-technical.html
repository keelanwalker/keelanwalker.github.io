What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

There are 3 controlled spacing areas between the content or an object and the next element. They are Margin, border and padding

  • Margins - are used to create space around elements outside of the borders.
  • Borders - are the area between the margin and the padding. A border has a number of different properties which it is possible to alter, ie width, and color and style.
  • Padding - is the area immediately around an object or content such as text or an image and is used to generate space around them inside of any border.

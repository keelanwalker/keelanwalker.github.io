Time and habits

Which Timebox and habits technique did you try?

I have only just read through the time and habit management resources and haven't actually used any of the techniques just yet. Of all of the techniques that I read through, I liked the 'Pomodoro Technique' the most because of it has a really clear, simple and practical process when it comes to implementing it into your work flow. I have seen a similar system used in a productivity diary that was created by a well known Youtuber/internet entrepreneur named Alex Icon, where he also suggested cutting work into 25 minute blocks separated with 5 minute breaks. There are obviously several benefits to doing this, especially when planning projects and estimating the length of time involved in completing it.

What did you find interesting?

Some of the early articles about mindfulness and meditation techniques were very interesting. This is not something I have practiced before or even experimented with. Given the results of some of the research that has been completed around meditation and was presented in the articles I have decided to commit 2 mins of meditation prior to each study session for 1 week and doubling it each week after that until I reach 10 mins.

Meditation Process

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

Before reading chapter 2, my thoughts about the topic were all based on my previous experiences exploring the concepts or practice of meditation. I have trialed similar methods in the past but never commited enough time to it in order to notice the benefits.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

In chapter 2 an example is given about a particular study which suggests there maybe a direct link between the increased antibody levels in a test subject who was vaccinated while meditating. This is not something I would have expected could be achieved through meditation and I'd be interested in seeing if further stuies have been done.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Not really, I think its about finding a comfortable position to support yourself so that it has the least affect on your meditation session.

Any other musings?

Yeh, although I believe meditation could be a really beneficial tool for personal development, I'm not quite ready to concede that some of the supernatural claims in chapter 2 are as valid as the author claims.
